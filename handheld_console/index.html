I nintendo-switch like made with HTML, CSS + JS

in brackets ("key") are buttons on a keyboard

Screen is filled with pixels that can be selected with Dpad (direction keys)

Power (1) - screen ON and OFF

Volume (r) - to toggle hide selected pixels

Left Trigger (z) and Right Trigger (x) - to shift pattern pointer left or right

Minus (q) and Plus (e) - to change color of screen bg + selector border or screen background depending on A

Dpad (Arrow Keys) - to active pixel selector on the screen

Interaction Buttons

W - to change selector Pattern (border radius)

D - to load/unload pre-set patterns which are switched by Triggers

S - to adjust pulsing effect on selector(s)

A - to shift color switcher between Screen + Border and pixel selector

Home (o) - to clear all pixels
